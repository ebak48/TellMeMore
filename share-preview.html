Button decisions
Primary"Sonucu paylaş" — violet fill. Shares /s/:shareId URL. navigator.share() mobile, clipboard desktop.
Secondary"Daha fazla kişiye sor" — ghost border. Copies /r/:profileSlug responder link. Different intent, lower hierarchy.
Privacy noteInline text only. No modal. Reminder, not blocker.
Share cardScreenshot-worthy HTML div. Dark bg, violet left bar. Contains only: name, headline, count, brand, URL.
/s/:shareIdPublic share teaser — what visitors see. Never exposes full result.
/r/:profileSlugResponder / invite link — answer about this person. 9-question flow.
shareId cachingCreated once on results load. Stored in state. Never re-generated on re-render.
PlacementBelow full results — not blocking result content. Natural endpoint of the results view.
Viral loop
1.User shares /s/:shareId link
2.Visitor sees teaser — no full result
3.Curiosity gap → visitor taps "Ben de söyleyeyim →"
4.Visitor completes 9-question responder flow
5.Visitor sees "Kendi profilini oluştur →"
6.Visitor creates profile → gets their own link → shares → loop
Result type — copy + visual matrix
Final approved copy for each type. Accent color, tone, and what to avoid.
Mystery
"Okumak zor. Ama kimse gözünü alamıyor."
Bar: dim violet #6d28d9. Tone: cool, incomplete, slightly unsettling. Short sentences. Never explain the mystery — the gap is the point.
Avoid: "gizemli" — too direct
Magnetic
"Ortama girince bir şeyler değişiyor. Farkında mısın?"
Bar: full violet. Tone: confident but incomplete. The question mark makes users want to know the answer. Never overstate.
Avoid: "çekici", "harika" — cheap
Contradiction
"İnsanlar sana doğal çekiliyor. Ama tam yaklaşmadan duruyor."
Bar: full violet. Most screenshot-worthy type. "Ama" is the pivot — never soften. Friends: "doğal çekiliyor" copy. Romance: "fark ediyor" copy — no attraction language.
Romance: never "çekici", "doğal çekiliyor"
Warm / Trusted
"Yanında olmak rahat. İnsanlar bunun farkında."
Bar: soft violet #a78bfa. Tone: anchoring, simple. "İnsanlar bunun farkında" adds social proof without being loud.
Avoid: "güvenilir" — HR language
Fun / Unpredictable
"Seninle olmak güzel. Ne yapacağın belli değil. İşte bu yüzden."
Bar: full violet. Three short sentences = rhythm. "İşte bu yüzden" lands unexpectedly. Implies energy without naming it.
Avoid: "eğlenceli" — overused
Low-data / Neutral
"Profil şekilleniyor. Ama bir şey zaten görünüyor."
Bar: muted #2a2a35. Tone: honest, forward-looking. Never show exact count below 5. "Ama bir şey" creates curiosity without false claim.
Avoid: exact low count visible
Romance mode — safety rules for copy
Never use: "attraction", "hot", "sexy", "crush", "romantic", "beğenilmek", "çekici bulmak"
Use instead: "insanlar daha fazlasını öğrenmek istiyor", "merak uyandırıyor", "yaklaşmak istiyor"
Suggestive, not explicit — the reader infers the romantic context
Mode label absent on public page — exposure risk removed
