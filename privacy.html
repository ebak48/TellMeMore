Privacy Policy

Effective: May 2026

What we collect

TellMeMore collects the first name you provide when creating a profile, the mode you select (Friends or Romance), and anonymous answers submitted by people you invite. We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, or any other identifying information.

Anonymity

Answers submitted about you are anonymous. We do not store or expose the identity of anyone who answers. Responders are identified only by a one-way hash — we cannot reverse this to find out who responded, and neither can you.

How your data is used

Your data is used solely to generate perception insights for your profile. We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for any purpose beyond the product itself.

Data storage

Data is stored in a secure database on Railway infrastructure. We use standard security practices. No sensitive personal data (passwords, payment information, government IDs) is ever collected.

Deleting your data

To delete your profile and all associated responses, contact us at the address below. We will remove your data within 7 days.

Cookies

TellMeMore does not use tracking cookies. We may use localStorage in your browser to remember your profile session. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to any third party.

Age

TellMeMore is intended for users 18 and older. By using the service, you confirm that you meet this requirement.

Changes

We may update this policy. If we make significant changes, we will update the effective date above.
